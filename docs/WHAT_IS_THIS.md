## What are "Earnings Call Transcripts"? ğŸ“

### Simple Explanation:

**Earnings Call** = A phone meeting where company executives talk about their financial performance

**Transcript** = The written version of everything said in that meeting

**Example:**
- Apple sells iPhones
- Every 3 months, they announce: "We sold X million iPhones"
- They hold a call to discuss the numbers
- Investors ask questions
- Everything is written down = Transcript

Companies like:
- Apple, Microsoft, Google (Technology)
- Amazon, Walmart (Retail)
- Tesla, Ford (Automotive)
- JPMorgan, Visa (Finance)

All publish their earnings call transcripts on websites like:
- The Motley Fool (fool.com)
- Seeking Alpha (seekingalpha.com)
- Company investor relations pages
